Sun

The Sun is the star at the centre of our solar system and is responsible for the Earth's climate and weather.

The sun is the most perfectly round natural object known in the universe,As a spinning ball of gas, astronomers had always expected our nearest star to bulge slightly at its equator, making it very slightly flying-saucer shaped.
